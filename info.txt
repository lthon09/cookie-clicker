COOKIE CLICKER
by lthon09

This is my very first game, so it may look ugly

The game is just about clicking a cookie, it's made in Python
Its upgrade system is simple, if your upgrade level is 0 you would get 1 Cookie per Click, leveling up the next level
would get you 2 Cookies per Click. To upgrade, you need a certain amount of Cookies

Just if you want to cheat, you can edit the data in the data.json file

Thanks for playing!
